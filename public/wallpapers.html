Geno7's Anime Wallpapers

Proper desktop wallpapers for animes I like have become weirdly harder and harder to find, so a lot of times, usually while procrastinating on something else in an executive dysfunction-induced fugue state, I'll take it upon myself to create a few of my own by making edits to official artwork. This page exists as a little place I can share what I've made with anyone who needs em. Might make updates to this occasionally if I'm feeling it but there are currently no plans to quit my day job and go pro with this or anything
